<h4>WELCOME</h4>

<img src="//images.neopets.com/items/toy_faeriebottleempty.gif" style="float: left; margin: 10px;"><p>
Welcome to the <b>Quirky Aesthetic 2.0 Arcade</b>! Each month, our guild travels to a different Neopian land to learn their customs and cultures, including their games! This month, we travel to the beautiful <b>Faerieland</b>, where we celebrate all the beauty and magic of the Faeries!
</p>

<p>Participating in this month's arcade will award guild members a <b>badge</b>, <b>guild points</b> just for submitting scores, and <b>guild points and prizes</b> for our top tournament scorers.</p>

<p>Post your scores to our <i>neoboard</i> by 11:59 PM NST every Saturday for a bonus weekly prize. Final submissions are due to <i>tifabee</i> by 11:59 PM NST three days after the last of month.</p>





<br></div>

<h4 id="howto">HOW TO PLAY</h4>

<p>Our arcade is split up into two sections: the tournament games, and the casual games. <b>Casual games</b> earn <i>guild points</i>. <b>Tournament games</b> earn <i>guild points</i> and <i>prizes</i>. Members are encouraged to participate however they feel comfortable. All participation in the arcade earns <i>guild badges</i>.
</p>

<p>Each week, post your scoresheet to the Quirky Aesthetic 2.0 Recruitment Board to earn guild points. Tournament game scores will be recorded and put on to the leaderboard. There is no need to report every score – only your <i>best</i> score.
</p>

<p>Submit your scores to tifabee no later than <b>three days</b> after the end of the month. Final tournament results will be posted by the <b>10th</b> of the <b>following month</b>.
</p>

<p>Faerie Bubbles pop if you get three of the same type together, and it's up to you to clear each level by popping all of them. Use the <b>left and right arrow keys</b> to move the bubble cannon and press the <b>space bar</b> to fire.<br>
If you get <b>four or more</b> bubbles together you will set off a combo. The first three are good combos, and the bottom three are bad combos.
</p><ul><li><b>Water</b> - This destroys all water bubbles on the screen.</li>
<li><b>Light</b> - This changes all bubbles touching the combo to a random colour.</li>
<li><b>Fire</b> - This burns all non-water bubbles that touch the combo. </li>
<li><b>Air</b> - This is BAD. Don't do an air combo, seriously. </li>
<li><b>Earth</b> - This makes a new line of bubbles appear. </li>
<li><b>Dark</b> - All the bubbles in the combo will turn to random types. </li>
</ul>
The less moves you complete each level in, the higher your score will be. Also look out for Nova and Rainbow bubbles, each with their own special effects!<br>
Just in case you didn't know - you can click the little <b>X</b> in the top left hand corner of the game board to get options such as turn music off and restart.
